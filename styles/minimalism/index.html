About This Design:

Minimalism is a rather popular design style that appears in many different places & mediums. You can find minimalistic inspirations in everything from graphic design to architecture.

Minimalism is all about less. Less is more. Don’t add that, instead, throw it out. When it comes to a minimalist lifestyle, this represents cutting down on your clutter and consuming less, but when not much changes when it comes to graphic design.

Avoid obtuse gradients, clean up your menus, let your shit breathe! No pushing, no overcrowding, no cutting in line. What, think those don’t have anything to do with designing products? Think again!

Minimalism is a challenge to get right. It’s not about putting too little content down, it’s about putting too little care into said product. Your product can be extremely bare & empty, but if you showcase the important features & only focus on what matters, it’ll come off looking good. Don’t just cut out content for the sake of cutting out content. Instead, use this new found space to highlight only the good stuff.

What is your product? Why should people care? Why is it special? These 3 questions will help you in determining exactly what to put, and where to put it.
